Difference between pie and bubble chart?


For pie chart generally any primary key or large values are not suitable, dates with exception of by year or quarters is not suitable
Anything more than 5-8 variation is not suitable

Discrete colors : Means all colors are separate from each other, no no shades.
					It is based on Dimensions
					
Continues colors : Based on Measures which are numeric so they have shading.

Percentages is always spread horizontally in tableau
