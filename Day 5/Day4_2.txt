COGS : Cost of Goods Sold

Difference between Bullet graph and Dual Combo Graph
-----------------------------------------------------

*Bullet graph does not require Date field
*Dual Combo requires Date field

+Dual Combo is used for time series analysis
+Bullet graph is suitable for non-Date dimensions

In bullet graph the dark shading around the bar indicates 60% of target and light shading indicates 80% and it is editable(not commonly so).
